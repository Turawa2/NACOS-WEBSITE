Hello There

We Are NACOSSITE

The Nigerian Association of Computing Science Students (NACOSS) at the Nigerian Institute of Leather and Science Technology (NILEST) is the ultimate hub for tech-savvy innovators and future tech leaders. We don’t just learn Computer Science – we live it, breathe it, and revolutionize it! NACOSS at NILEST is where dreams in programming, AI, app development, and tech innovation come to life. With cutting-edge workshops, thrilling hackathons, and industry-level projects, we prepare our members to dominate the tech world. At NILEST, NACOSS isn’t just a department – it’s a powerhouse of creativity, innovation, and excellence. Ready to make waves in the tech space? Join us, and let’s turn your tech dreams into reality! #NILESTNACOSSITE
